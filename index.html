How it works

Pick a music specialist

Choose the genre that fits her. Hip hop, jazz, country, R&B, Latin, rock, indie, pop, or electronic. Each specialist has their own style.

Tell them about her

Share her name, her personality, her story. Every detail you share makes the song more unique. You'll then be redirected to register on app.napster.com to complete your song.
